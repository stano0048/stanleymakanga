Frequently Asked Questions

You can reach out via email, phone, or the contact form on this page. I recommend scheduling a consultation call to discuss your project requirements in detail. After understanding your needs, I'll provide a proposal with timelines and costs.

My rates vary depending on project complexity, scope, and timeline. I offer both fixed-price contracts for well-defined projects and hourly rates for ongoing work. After discussing your project details, I'll provide a transparent quote with no hidden fees.
